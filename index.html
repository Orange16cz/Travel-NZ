Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit New Zealand?

New Zealand is a year-round destination. Summer (December-February) is ideal for outdoor activities and beach visits. Autumn (March-May) offers beautiful foliage and fewer crowds. Winter (June-August) is perfect for skiing and snow sports. Spring (September-November) features blooming flowers and newborn wildlife.

Do I need a visa to visit New Zealand?

Visa requirements depend on your nationality. Citizens of many countries can visit New Zealand for up to 3 months without a visa. However, it's essential to check the latest requirements with the New Zealand Immigration Service before booking your trip.

What is the cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy allows for full refunds up to 30 days before your tour date. Cancellations between 15-30 days before the tour receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 14 days of the tour are non-refundable. Some specialized tours may have different policies, which will be clearly stated during booking.

Are your tours suitable for children and seniors?

Yes, we offer tours suitable for all ages. Each tour description includes information about physical requirements and age recommendations. We also offer private tours that can be customized to accommodate specific needs and preferences.
